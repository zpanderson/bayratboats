Boat Rentals

Bay Rat Boat rentals offer you the opportunity to explore the scenic beauty of Aransas Pass, Texas, and its surrounding waters at your leisure. Whether you're an avid angler, a nature enthusiast, or a family seeking some fun in the sun, we have the perfect vessel for your needs.

Each of our boats is well maintained and designed to provide a safe and comfortable experience on the water. Whether you're planning a solo expedition, a romantic getaway, or a family outing, Bay Rat Boats is your premier destination for unforgettable memories on the water. So, grab your sunscreen, don your captain's hat, and embark on your next adventure with Bay Rat Boats!

Pricing

  • Half Day - (4 hours) - $200 (7am - 11am or 12pm - 4pm)
  • Full Day - (9 hours) - $300 (7am - 4pm)

Boat Details

Our fleet consists of 16’ Carolina Skiffs, each equipped with reliable 15 hp Yamaha outboard engines.

These versatile boats are renowned for their durability, stability, and maneuverability, making them ideal for navigating the coastal waters of Aransas Pass.

Hours

Our hours are currently by appointment only. Please call or email us to schedule your rental.

Safety

Must be 21 or over to rent but all ages are welcome along.

When you rent a boat with us, rest assured that all necessary safety equipment will be provided for your peace of mind. From life jackets to navigation lights, we ensure that you have everything you need to enjoy a safe and worry-free boating experience.

Additionally, each rental comes with a tank of gas to get you started on your adventure. Please note that for your safety and protection, a liability waiver is required before embarking on your journey.

We value your safety above all else and want to ensure that your time on the water is both enjoyable and secure.
