Welcome to Bay Rat Boats!

Located in the heart of Aransas Pass, TX, Bay Rat Boats invites you to embark on your own maritime adventure. Whether you're looking to cast a line and reel in the catch of the day or simply enjoy a leisurely cruise on the open water, we've got the perfect boat for you.

At Bay Rat Boats, you are the captain of your own journey. Our well-maintained fleet is ready for you to take the helm, providing the freedom and excitement that only a day on the water can offer. With no special license required, it's never been easier to set sail and create unforgettable memories.

Join us at Bay Rat Boats and experience the joy of boating like never before. The water is calling - let's get you out there!

Boat Details

Our fleet consists of 16 foot Carolina Skiffs, each equipped with reliable 15 hp Yamaha outboard engines. These models come with an electric start system, ensuring easy and reliable starting every time.

Our boats have a spacious deck layout that has ample room for movement, seating, and storage. Each boat is able to hold up to four people or approximately 600 pounds.

Because of the size and speed of our boats, no special boating license is required to rent a Bay Rat Boat, but you must have a valid drivers license.

Hours

7 days a week - 7am - 5pm

Please call, text, or email us to secure your spot, but walk-ups are welcome if we have boats available.

Safety

Must be 21 or over to rent but all ages are welcome along.

We value your safety above all else and want to ensure that your time on the water is both enjoyable and secure. For this reason, a liability waiver is required before embarking on your journey. Please make sure to arrive approximately 15 minutes earlier than your departure time to fill this out.

Every boat rental comes with all the needed equipment to run the boat safely and effectively:

  • Lifejackets for every person on board
  • Throwable cushion
  • Anchor
  • Push pole & paddle
  • Fire extinguisher & whistle
  • Full tank of gas with no additional fuel surcharge

At the time of rental, we will give you a tutorial on safe operation of the boat, as well as ensuring familiarity with the surroundings and how to troubleshoot any issues that could arise.

Who We Are

Welcome to Bay Rat Boats, where family, tradition, and a love for the water come together. Our founder, Brant Nugent, has proudly served in the United States Coast Guard for nearly 20 years. His dedication to service is matched only by his commitment to his family and his passion for boating.

Brant balances his military career with family life alongside his wonderful wife, Sarah. When you call Bay Rat Boats, Sarah is typically the friendly voice you'll hear on the other end of the line. Together, they are raising four amazing daughters: Isabelle, Allyson, Emilie, and Abigail.

Bay Rat Boats is a true family business. The inspiration for our venture came when Brant purchased four 16' Carolina Skiffs from his dad and decided to share the joy of boating by renting them out. Since then, we've grown into a close-knit team where everyone plays a role. Brant ensures the boats are in tip-top shape; Sarah handles the phones and technology; and the kids chip in wherever they can. Grandparents are always ready to help with the children, and Grandpa, with his wealth of knowledge about boats and maintenance, ensures we all learn from the best.

At Bay Rat Boats, we believe in the power of family and community. We're here to help you create unforgettable memories on the water, just as we do. Join us and become part of our extended family.
